New York State's Brownfield Opportunity Areas Program

The BOA program is a planning program with its roots in the environmental justice movement. It targets poor communities that have large clusters of brownfields. Through extensive community outreach, the BOA program aims to give underserved communities a voice and a vision for the future of their neighborhoods.
